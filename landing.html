About Blossoms

Blossoms is the annual cultural festival of Christ University, Bangalore. It is a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity, and cultural diversity. In this event, students from all departments showcase their talents by participating as a group or individually. All non-participants and parents are requested to attend this event where there will be a lot of fun and entertainment.
